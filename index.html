About

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Economics and a Research Fellow at Universidad del Pacífico. I hold a PhD in Economics and MSc degrees in Research in Economics and Economics of Aging Finance, all from Tilburg University (Netherlands). I also earned a bachelor’s degree in Economics from Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú.

My research focuses on public policies, pensions, and labor markets. I have advised governments in Peru, the Netherlands, and Latin America, and my work has been published in leading international journals and policy reports. I am also the author of a book proposing pension system reforms in Peru.

Previously, I served as a consultant for the Inter-American Development Bank, a researcher at the Ministry of Social Affairs in the Netherlands, and a senior economist at BBVA and Peru’s Ministry of Economy and Finance.
